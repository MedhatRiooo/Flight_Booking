About Us

Our History

Qatar Airways was established on November 22, 1993. At that time, the airline operated only a small fleet of aircraft to a limited number of destinations. Since then, Qatar Airways has grown to become one of the world's leading airlines, with a modern fleet of over 200 aircraft serving more than 160 destinations worldwide.

We are committed to providing our passengers with the highest level of service, comfort, and convenience. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, we strive to make your journey as enjoyable as possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the airline of choice for travelers around the world. We are dedicated to providing our passengers with a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable travel experience, while also contributing to the economic growth and development of our country.

We are committed to operating our business in a responsible and sustainable manner, and we are constantly working to reduce our environmental impact and promote social and economic development in the communities we serve.
